Chicken Ramen

Description

I found this recipe online, it is delicious! It surely beats instant ramen both in healthiness and taste. Try it today in about 30 minutes!

Ingredients

For the soup:

  • 1 tbsp oil (I like olive or seasame)
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 tbsp ginger, grated
  • 4 cloves garlic, pressed
  • 1 tbsp chili garlic sauce
  • 2 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tbsp fish sauce
  • 1/2 cup low sodium soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup rice vinegar
  • 4-8oz shiitake mushrooms, stemmed and sliced
  • 1/2-3/4 pounds bok choy, cubed
  • 1 lb boneless skinless chicken thighs
  • 4 cups low sodium chicken broth
  • 1 cup water
  • 1-2 packages soba noodles, prepared according to packages instructions

For the eggs:

  • 4 eggs
  • 1/4 cup low sodium soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup rice vinegar
  • 3/4 cup water

Optional toppings

  • Green onion, sliced
  • Cilantro
  • Chili garlic sauce
  • Sesame seeds
  • Lime wedges

Instructions

To prepare the ramen egg

  1. Whisk together the soy sauce, rice vinegar, and water, set aside.
  2. Prepare and ice bath for the eggs by filling a bowl with ice and water.
  3. Bring a pot of water to boil. Carefully lower the eggs into the boiling water using a slotted spoon. Reduce heat if necessary to keep the water just barely boiling. Cook for 7 minues, then remove from pot and place eggs in ice bath. Leave in the ice bath for 3 minutes. Remove from ice bath and gently peel (the eggs will be soft). Place peeled eggs in the marinade. Leave in the marinade for several hours or overnight.

To prepare the soup

  1. Heat a large pot or dutch oven over medium high heat. Add the onion and saute until starting to soften, about 5 minutes. Add the mushrooms and saute for another 2-3 minutes. Add the garlic and ginger and stir fry for about 30 seconds or until fragrant. Add the chicken broth and water, soy sauce,rice vinegar, oyster sauce, fish sauce, and chili garlic sauce, stir to combine.
  2. When broth reaches a simmer, add the chicken thighs. Simmer for 15-20 minutes until the chicken is cooked through and reaches internal temp of 165F.
  3. Remove the chicken and shred using two forks. Return to broth along with the bok choy and eggs to reheat and cook for 3-5 minutes. Remove from heat.
  4. To serve, place a serving of soba noodles in a bowl, then pour soup over them. Top ramen with egg and desired additional toppings.
