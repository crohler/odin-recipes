Chicken Broccoli Casserole

Description

This dish is one of my wife's favorites. Cheesy, savory, and with a crisp cracker topping it is sure to please!

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 lb chicken breast, cut into bite size pieces
  • 1 tbsp italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 1/2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 1/4 cups white rice
  • 2 cups broccoli
  • 2 cans cream of chicken soup
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 2 cups cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup ritz crackers, crushed

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350F.
  2. Mix chicken with seasonings.
  3. Melt butter in medium fry pan. Add chicken and cook until golden
  4. Combine broth, olive oil, and rice in a pot. Bring to boil then reduce heat to simmer. Cover tightly and cook for 6 minutes.
  5. Remove from heat, add broccoli, chicken, cream of chicken soup, sour cream, and half of the cheddar cheese to the pot. Stir well.
  6. Pour mixture into a greased 9x13" baking dish.
  7. Cover and bake for 15 minutes.
  8. Remove cover and add the crushed crackers on top of casserole. Return to oven, uncovered for 10 more minutes.
  9. Allow finished casserole to sit for 5 minutes, then serve.
